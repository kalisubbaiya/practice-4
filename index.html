A strategic placement for the kids' corner

A play corner can offer benefits for many different areas in your hotel. At the restaurant you give guests the opportunity to dine quietly and extensively without the children getting bored. And, a challenging play corner in the lobby ensures that your staff has more time and attention for your guests while the children can enjoy themselves during the wait.By choosing a strategic location for the play corner, for example between the lobby and the restaurant, the guests and staff will optimally experience the positive effect. Happy children ensure happy parents.
